LEAGUE ESSENTIALS

Your most answered question answered. Questions? Email batcitysoccer@gmail.com

Bat City Soccer 7s is an adult soccer league in Austin offering 7v7 games on artificial turf under lights. It's designed for players of all skill levels who want to enjoy the sport in a fun and competitive environment.

You can register by visiting our LeagueApps portal. Select your preferred location and schedule, and follow the registration instructions.

Games are held midweek in the evenings at various locations in Austin, including Northeast Metro Park, Round Rock, and South Austin. Specific days and times are available on our registration portal.

The fee covers referee fees, team jerseys, game balls, an 8-game season (6 group games plus 2 playoff games), and entry into select travel tournaments for winning teams of competitive divisions.

Yes, individual players can join as free agents. We will place you on a team, which is a great option if you don't have enough players to form your own team.

Our leagues cater to all skill levels, from novice to advanced. You can choose the level that best matches your experience and abilities.

Yes, we sometimes offer an early bird discount. Check our registration portal for current promotions and discount codes.

Bring your soccer gear, including cleats and shin guards. We provide team jerseys and game balls. Make sure to arrive early to warm up before the game.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please email us at batcitysoccer@gmail.com. We're here to help and ensure you have a great experience.

You can stay updated by visiting our website, following us on social media, and subscribing to our newsletter for the latest news, schedules, and promotions.
